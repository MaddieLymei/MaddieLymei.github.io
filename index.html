ABOUT

Animal Lover

Madeleine absolutely loves animals. She went vegeterian for about 2 years, and owns four cats and one dog. All her animals are adopted, and she constantly has animals in foster care. To see if there are any animals available for adoption in her foster care, check out the gallery!

An organisation that she collaborates with, and fosters for often is Woodrock Animal Rescue.


Nature Enthusiast

One of Madeleine's goals this year is to try see and experience as much of nature as Africa has to offer. She really got into hiking when she lived in England in 2021. She is looking to conquer KZN, Western Cape, Gauteng and Limpopo's top hiking trails by the end of the year. One hike she is hoping to complete is Rota Vicentina, in Portugal.

Madeleine is almost done with her honours degree in Software Engineering. Her research topic was titled "The use of computer visioning in Plastic Recycling, South Africa", she has done her BComp and honours through Belgium Campus. She hopes to complete her masters in drone technology, implementing Artificial Intelligence to reduce deforestation and human rights violations in the Congo.


Philanthropist

A heart that breaks everytime someone suffers. One day she hopes to see a change in the world by initiating it herself and through the company that she works for. If everyone put a little bit of effort in, she believes that the world could be a better place.

Madeleine's past and present projects:

  • Past Project: Working with a tetraplegic in England with daily care.
  • Past Project: Working with an elderly man of 88 years on, who has Parkinsons disease, in England with daily care.
  • Current Project: Helping an under privildged child (Gresham) go to school by partially paying for fee's and providing transportation.
